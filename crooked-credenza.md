1. Brand Bio (Website “About”)

Crooked Credenza is an interior design studio and curatorial platform rooted in the belief that beauty doesn’t require perfection.

We design spaces that feel lived-in, layered, and deeply personal — embracing irregularity, patina, and the quiet character that comes from time, use, and individuality.

Rather than erasing flaws, we design around them. We value homes that evolve slowly, objects that carry history, and interiors that reflect real lives rather than idealized images.

Alongside interior design services, Crooked Credenza curates and supports designers, brands, and artisans we genuinely love — pieces chosen for the humanity, craftsmanship, impact, and lasting presence.

This is design that leaves room for life.

2. Soft Manifesto

We believe homes should feel inhabited.

That asymmetry can be beautiful.
That wear tells a story.
That character matters more than polish.

We believe design should support life — not compete with it.

Crooked Credenza is about working with what exists, honoring flaws, and creating spaces that feel personal, collected, and quietly confident.

3. Ideal Client Definition (For You + Marketing)

Crooked Credenza clients are:

Emotionally driven, not trending-driven
Drawn to art,travel, fashion, and culture
Comfortable mixing old and new
More interested in feeling than perfection
Often creatives, collectors, or thoughtful professionals

They are not:

Chasing Pinterest replicas
Interested in fast, disposable design
Obsessed with matching sets or showroom finishes

What they want:

“I want my home to feel like me — not like a model home.”

4. Interior Design Services

Crooked Credenza offers interior design rooted in authenticity, layering, and restraint. Our approach balances refinement with imperfection, creating spaces thats that feel intentional yet relaxed, curated yet deeply personal.

Services include:

Full-service residential interior design
Room-by-room design & refreshes
Floor Plans and realistic renderings
Furniture layout and sourcing
Vintage & antique integration
Styling and object curation
Finish and material selection

Each project begins with listening — understanding how you live, what you love, and what already holds meaning.

5. Design Philosophy

Our approach:

We mix eras, conditions, and price points
We welcome patina, wear, irregularity
We avoid over-styling and trend saturation
We value restraint, balance, and longevity

We believe the most compelling interiors are never finished — only lived in.
